Which Plan Is Right for You?

Match your role and needs to the best plan below.

🏢

Team Standard Seat

  • Business teams of 5–150 people
  • Marketing, HR, Sales, Operations staff
  • Teams that collaborate on shared AI projects
  • Organizations needing SSO & admin control
  • Those who don't need heavy daily coding
  • Budget-conscious teams wanting enterprise features
⚙️

Team Premium Seat

  • Lead engineers within company teams
  • Power users who hit Standard limits
  • Developers needing Claude Code in a managed org
  • Teams where a few people need far more usage
  • Mix with Standard seats to save cost
  • Those needing 6.25× more capacity than Pro
🚀

Max Individual

  • Freelancers & solo developers/founders
  • Power users who hit Pro limits weekly/daily
  • Writers producing long-form content daily
  • Researchers analyzing huge documents
  • Those who don't need team collaboration features
  • Developers who want latest features first
🏛️

Enterprise

  • Large corporations with 150+ employees
  • Regulated industries: finance, healthcare, legal
  • Government agencies with compliance needs
  • Teams that need HIPAA, audit logs, SCIM
  • Orgs that want usage billed like a utility (pay per token)
  • Companies needing custom MSAs & dedicated support

Pay-per-use pricing for developers building apps on Claude. Priced per million tokens (≈ 750,000 words). Input = what you send. Output = what Claude replies. Cache = reusing repeated context cheaply.
